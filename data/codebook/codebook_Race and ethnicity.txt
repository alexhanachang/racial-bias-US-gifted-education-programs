Race and ethnicity data

Description
Race and ethnicity

Usage
RE

Format
Data frame with columns

COMBOKEY
Primary key. Represents a unique school.

state
State (abbreviated).

race
"Race and ethnicity broken into six categories: asian, black, hisp, white, other, and bipoc."

gat_count
Number of students in Gifted and Talented program.

race_count
Number of students by race and ethnicity.

gat_total
Total number of students in Gifted and Talented program at school.

total
Total number of students at school.
