Summation by state data

Description
Summation by state

Usage
RE

Format
Data frame with columns

state
State (abbreviated).

race
Race and ethnicity broken into two categories: white and bipoc.

gat_count
Number of students in Gifted and Talented program.

race_count
Number of students by race and ethnicity.

bipoc_white_ratio
Ratio of BIPOC to white students in Gifted and Talented program.

Source
"U.S. Department of Education, CRDC."
https://www2.ed.gov/about/offices/list/ocr/docs/2017-18-crdc-data.zip
